Jared Kushner

Financial disclosures

Jared Kushner is a real estate investor and developer, and a senior advisor to President Donald Trump. He is also married to the President's daughter, Ivanka Trump, with whom he has 3 children.

White House Role

Under President Trump, Kushner holds "one of the most expansive roles in the White House." He has been assigned sweeping responsibilities within the Trump administration, including:

  • heading the new White House Office of American Innovation
  • veterans care
  • tackling the opioid epidemic
  • foreign relations
  • criminal justice reform
  • brokering peace in the Middle East

(See CNN for details.)

Business Ties

According to recent White House disclosures, as reported by ProPublica, Kushner and his wife, Ivanka Trump, had over $700 million assets at the start of their White House tenure. Much of this originates from Kushner Companies, of which Mr. Kushner was the chief executive until January. Over the past 10 years, Kushner companies has participated in over $7 billion of acquisitions. Until recently, Kushner also held positions in 267 business entities in New York, New Jersey, and Washington, D.C.
